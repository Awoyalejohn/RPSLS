How to play

This is a version of the classic game; rock, paper, scissors. It adds hand gestures for lizard and spock. Which causes the game to have more outcomes and reduces the chance of drawing with your opponent. Game is still very simple, just select one of the hand gestures below and the computer will responds with its own choice. The winner will be declared based on these outcomes: Scissors cuts Paper, Paper covers Rock, Rock crushes Lizard, Lizard poisons Spock, Spock smashes Scissors, Scissors decapitates Lizard, Lizard eats Paper, Paper disproves Spock, Spock vaporizes Rock, (and as it always has) Rock crushes Scissors. You win the game if you won in 5 rounds, lose if you lost in 5 rounds, and draw if you drew in 5 rounds. Which ever happens first.
